Healthcare Schemes
  • Public Health is a State subject; hence, the responsibility of providing medical assistance to patients of all income group is of respective State/ UT Governments. However, National Health Mission (NHM) – a flagship programme of the Ministry with its two Sub-Missions, National Rural Health Mission (NRHM) and National Urban Health Mission (NUHM), supports States /UTs to strengthen their health care systems so as to provide universal access to equitable, affordable and quality health care services.

Educational Schemes
  • The Indian government has launched various schemes and initiatives to improve the quality of education in the country. These schemes are aimed at providing equal access to education for all children, regardless of their socioeconomic background.The Indian government is continuously working towards the betterment of the education Admission Management in the country and introducing new schemes to improve the education of the students. However, it is important to note that the implementation of these schemes varies across states

Agricultural Schemes
  • Agricultural schemes in India play a pivotal role in addressing various challenges faced by the agricultural sector, such as low productivity, inadequate infrastructure, and lack of access to modern technology. These schemes aim to enhance farmers' income, ensure food security, promote sustainable farming practices, and empower rural communities. By providing financial assistance, subsidies, and technical support, these schemes contribute to the overall development of agriculture and rural livelihoods.
