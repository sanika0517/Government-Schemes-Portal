Women Helpline

 
        The Women Helpline Scheme is an initiative by the Government of India to provide 24/7 emergency response to women affected by violence or in distress. The helpline aims to 
        offer immediate support, guidance, and assistance to women facing various forms of violence, including domestic violence, harassment, trafficking, and more.

Objectives of Women Helpline

  1. Emergency Response: The helpline offers immediate response and assistance to women facing violence, including rescue, medical assistance, legal support, and counseling services.
  2. Counseling and Guidance: Trained counselors are available to provide emotional support, guidance, and counseling to women experiencing various forms of violence or distress.
  3. Referral Services: The helpline connects women with appropriate support services, including shelters, legal aid, medical facilities, and rehabilitation services, as per their specific needs.

Eligibility Criteria:

  1. The Women Helpline Scheme primarily targets women and girls who are victims of violence, harassment, abuse, or facing any form of distress.
  2. Age Criteria:There is no specific age limit mentioned; however, the helpline services are available for women and girls of all ages who require assistance.
  3. Nature of Distress: Women facing various forms of violence, including domestic violence, sexual harassment, trafficking, acid attacks, dowry-related harassment, and other forms of gender-based violence, are eligible to seek assistance from the helpline.
  4. Location: The Women Helpline Scheme is accessible across India, and women from all states and union territories can avail of the services by dialing the designated helpline number.

Key Components:

  1. Immediate Assistance: The helpline offers prompt response and assistance to women facing various forms of violence, including domestic violence, sexual harassment, trafficking, acid attacks, dowry-related harassment, and more.
  2. Rescue and Medical Assistance: In critical situations, the helpline coordinates with local authorities and medical facilities to provide immediate rescue and medical assistance to women in distress.
  3. Counselors: The helpline employs trained counselors who offer emotional support, guidance, and counseling to women experiencing trauma, distress, or violence.
  4. Confidentiality: The counseling services prioritize confidentiality, ensuring women feel safe and secure while sharing their experiences and seeking guidance.
