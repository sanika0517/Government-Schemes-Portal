Manjhi Kanya Bhagyashree Scheme

 A scheme for empowerment of the Girl child by the Department of Women and Child Development, Maharashtra. 
The scheme was launched on 1st August 2017.

Objectives of Manjhi Kanya Bhagyashree Scheme

  1. To promote the birth rate of girl children in the state.
  2. To ensure access to education, healthcare, and nutrition for girls.
  3. To empower girls by encouraging their participation in various educational
  4. To enroll girls in various national skill development programs.
  5. To reduce the incidence of child marriage.

Eligibility Criteria:

  1. who have girl children born after 1st August 2017
  2. having annual income upto 7.5 Lakhs
  3. After submission of Family Planning Certificate

Key Components:

  1. One Girl Child: Rs.50,000 for a period of 18 years
  2. Two Girl Children: Rs. 25,000 each on the name of both the Girls
  3. The families can withdraw accumulated interest after every six years.
