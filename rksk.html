Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram 

 
    The Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram (RKSK) is an initiative by the Government of India aimed at addressing the health and development needs of adolescents 
 in the country. Here are some details and eligibility criteria based on the information available up to that time:

Objectives of RKSK:

  1. Promotion of healthy behaviors: Encourage adolescents to adopt healthy lifestyles.
  2. Prevention and control of nutritional deficiencies: Address issues like anemia, malnutrition, and other health problems.
  3. Sexual and reproductive health: Educate adolescents about reproductive health, family planning, and safe sexual practices.
  4. Substance misuse prevention: Raise awareness about the harmful effects of substance abuse.
  5. Injuries and violence prevention: Promote safety measures and awareness about the risks of injuries and violence.

Criteria:

The RKSK primarily targets adolescents between the ages of 10 and 19 years, although specific eligibility criteria may vary based on the specific components of the program. Generally, adolescents from both rural and urban areas are covered under this scheme.

Key Components:

  1. Adolescent Health Days: These are organized to provide health services, counseling, and education to adolescents.
  2. Weekly Iron and Folic Acid Supplementation (WIFS): To address nutritional deficiencies, particularly anemia.
  3. Life Skills Education: Providing adolescents with skills related to communication, decision-making, and coping with emotions.
  4. Mental Health: Addressing the mental health needs of adolescents through counseling and awareness programs.
  5. Preventive Health Services: Vaccination, deworming, and other preventive health measures are also part of the program.
  6. Implementation:The RKSK is implemented through various channels, including schools, health facilities, and community-based organizations. Multi-sectoral collaboration involving education, health, and other relevant departments is crucial for the effective implementation of the program.
