Manodhairya Yojana 

 
        Manodhairya Yojana is a scheme initiated by the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, to provide financial assistance and support to victims of rape
        and acid attack. The scheme aims to provide immediate and long-term support to help survivors regain their confidence and live a dignified life.

Objectives of Manodhairya Yojana

  1. Financial Assistance: Under this scheme, victims of rape and acid attack are provided with financial assistance to cover medical, legal, and rehabilitation expenses.
  2. Counseling and Support Services: The scheme also offers psychological counseling and support services to help survivors cope with the trauma and rebuild their lives.
  3. Legal Support: Manodhairya Yojana provides legal support to victims by assisting them in navigating the criminal justice system, including filing complaints, attending court proceedings, and accessing legal aid.
  4. Rehabilitation and Livelihood Support: The scheme aims to facilitate the rehabilitation of survivors by providing them with vocational training, skill development programs, and employment opportunities.

    5. Eligibility Criteria:

    1. Victims of Rape: Women victims of rape, including minors, are eligible for financial assistance and support under this scheme.
    2. Victims of Acid Attack:Individuals who have suffered acid attacks are eligible for financial assistance and support under the Manodhairya Yojana.
    3. Age Criteria:There is no specific age limit mentioned in the scheme; however, the primary focus is on providing support to victims of rape and acid attack, irrespective of their age.
    4. Documentation:Victims are required to provide relevant documents, such as medical reports, FIR copy, and other supporting documents, to avail of the benefits under the scheme.

    Key Components:

    1. Expenses: The scheme provides financial assistance to cover the medical expenses incurred by the victim, including hospitalization, surgeries, medications, and other related treatments.
    2. Legal Expenses: Victims are supported financially to meet the expenses related to legal procedures, including filing complaints, attending court proceedings, and accessing legal aid.
    3. Rehabilitation and Livelihood Support: Financial assistance is provided to facilitate the rehabilitation of survivors, including vocational training, skill development programs, and employment opportunities.
    4. Psychological Counseling: Manodhairya Yojana offers psychological counseling services to help survivors cope with the trauma, emotional distress, and mental health issues arising from the incidents of rape or acid attack.
