Balika Samriddhi Yojana 

 "Balika Samriddhi Yojana," which was launched by the Government of India to address the welfare and development of the girl child. The program is aimed at promoting the 
enrollment and retention of girls in schools, improving their health and nutritional status, and supporting their overall well-being.

Objectives of Balika Samriddhi Yojana

  1. Promote Education:Encourage the enrollment and retention of girls in schools to promote education and skill development.
  2. Improve Health and Nutrition:Enhance the health and nutritional status of the girl child through support and interventions.
  3. Empower Girls:Empower girls with education and skills, enabling them to make informed choices and contribute to their communities.
  4. Address Gender Disparities:Address gender disparities by promoting the value and well-being of the girl child.

Eligibility Criteria:

  1. Family Income:The eligibility criteria often consider the family's income, and the benefits are typically targeted at families with lower socio-economic status.
  2. Girl Child:Families with a girl child may be eligible for various benefits under the scheme.
  3. School Enrollment:Some components of the scheme may be linked to the enrollment of the girl child in school.
